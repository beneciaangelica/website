Research Projects

This project explores the role of IL-18 binding protein in modulating inflammatory responses. I cloned and overexpressed IL-18BP in E. coli and am optimizing purification for functional studies.

I investigated whether owl monkey kidney (OMK) cells could be adapted to suspension culture. Though the attempt failed, it helped me better understand adhesion mechanisms and troubleshooting experimental conditions.

During my time at Kalsec, I worked on natural product stability and antioxidant testing using Oxidative Stability Index (OSI) assays. I also contributed to flavor compound research in the R&D lab.
